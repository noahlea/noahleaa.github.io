Who Am I?

          My name is Christian Noah G. Lea, 20 years old from Makati City and Bacoor Cavite. I was born in Pasay City which I grew up there until I am 5 years old. When I am studying in pre-school we left Pasay City and transfer to Parañaque so that my mother will have an easier route to her work. My mother's work is a Supervisor in a factory and my father's work is a Seaman. We transfer again to Cavite because we already bought a land there few years before I was born. And when I reach college I left Cavite and lived in Makati here with my uncle for me to have an easier transportation to my college school. I thought my life is very congusing because of these so much transfer and transfer to another place, but I was wrong this is what life is, you adjust for you to live, life is very hard to cope with but if you try to understand how these thing happened then you should not be confused. I think life is composed of ups and downs, you do things, you fail, then you do it again. I'm a type of person who never give up on things even in the hard part of my life because if you think that you can't do it then what is the purpose of you liking it.

Education is the best investment

            For me education aside from school is about what you learn from someone else or somethings and what you earn for your knowledge. But I think a person that is educated is the best if it is mixed with being hardworking in life because first of all education is nothing if you dont work hard for yourself. You cannot use knowledge if you are not a hardworking person, but sometimes I also believe that you can work smart if you think that things can be done in easier that a harder task.It is true that the best investment is education because let's be real without education life will hard for you, how can you understand how things happen you don't know how to live or live alone. You cannot invest on things to earn money if you don't have any knowledge and therefore you must invest on your knowledge or education first.
